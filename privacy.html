Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2024

Overview

LUMA OS is designed with privacy as a core principle. All speech recognition and voice processing happens locally on your computer.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

  • No voice data is sent to cloud servers
  • No telemetry or usage statistics are collected
  • No personal information is transmitted
  • All processing happens offline on your device

Local Data Storage

LUMA OS stores the following data locally on your computer:

  • Voice command history (stored locally in ~/.lumaos/context/)
  • System configuration files
  • Application logs

You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time.

Open Source

LUMA OS is fully open source. You can inspect the code to verify our privacy claims.
